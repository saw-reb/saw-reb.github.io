CHARACTER ARCHIVE

PERSONALITY

Leonard is a no-nonsense soldier with one goal in mind at all times: to get his job done. He's not a fan of needless violence and will only attack if necessary, but when he does attack, you better watch out! He's also easily annoyed, and hates when his party members dilly-dally for no reason. He can be a bit stuffy at times, but as long as you're on his side, he will defend you to the very end.

STORY

Leonard is unique from the other characters in this archive due to his limited nature. He was created for a dungeon-crawling one-shot, but has become so much more than that. During our dungeon crawl, our party encountered a mysterious and magical fountain. It wasn't until one of our players saved the life of another in a selfless act that the fountain granted us one wish. What did Leonard wish for? Well, to ascend to godhood, of course! He instantly jumped to level 20 and is now a revered deity of his hometown.

RACE: Tabaxi

CLASS: Level 20 Paladin

ALIGNMENT: Lawful Good
